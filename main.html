Rampant mark inflation in NEET — 715 was top score 2 yrs ago, now it can mean 225th rank

Consider this: NEET candidates who scored 715 marks this year received a rank of 225. In 2022, the topper had the same score. The situation comes as no surprise given that 67 candidates have tallied a perfect score of 720 this year.
