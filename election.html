India's Priyanka Gandhi to finally make electoral debut

Priyanka Gandhi, sister of India's main opposition Congress leader Rahul Gandhi, is set to contest her first election, ending decades of anticipation by her supporters. Ms Gandhi is a descendant of the Nehru-Gandhi family, India's most famous political dynasty, and her electoral debut will be closely watched. The 52-year-old will contest the Wayanad seat in the southern Indian state of Kerala after her brother relinquishes it. A win for Ms Gandhi would mean the presence of all three Gandhi family members in the Indian parliament.
